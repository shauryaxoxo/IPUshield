GUIDELINES FOR EMERGENCY SITUATION!

  1. Know emergency contact information: Save important phone numbers, such as campus security, local law enforcement, and emergency services, in your phone or keep them easily accessible.
  2. Follow evacuation procedures: Familiarize yourself with evacuation routes and assembly points on campus. In the event of a fire, explosion, or any situation requiring evacuation, calmly exit the building and proceed to the designated safe area.
  3. Stay alert and informed: Pay attention to any emergency alerts or notifications from the campus administration. These may be sent through text messages, emails, or loudspeaker announcements.
  4. Report emergencies: If you witness an emergency situation, promptly report it to campus security or emergency services. Provide them with accurate details about the incident, location, and any individuals involved.
  5. Stay calm and supportive: Remaining calm during an emergency can help maintain a sense of order and reduce panic. Offer support to those around you and follow the instructions of emergency personnel to ensure a safe and efficient response.
